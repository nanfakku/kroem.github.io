Character Lore

Susano-o is based on the Japanese god of the same name. Here is some background provided by the wiki.

Susano-o is a tyrant among the gods of Japanese mythology. Born two Izanami and Izanagi, he was originally known for being a violent and unruly being, he would find redemption inlater deeds of heroism. Susano-o would go to slay Yamata-no-Orochi and by doing so, save kushinada whom he would marry later on. Shortly after, he would present the sword of Kusanagi to his sister Amaterasu. A sword crafted from the tail of Orochi.-Megami Tensei Wiki
